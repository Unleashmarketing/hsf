Your Journey to Germany

Our structured 6-step process ensures successful placement and seamless integration.

1

Application Review

Submit your medical qualifications and career preferences. We conduct thorough evaluation and assess compatibility with German standards.

2

Professional Assessment

Comprehensive interviews in Portuguese, English, and German. We evaluate medical knowledge and cultural adaptability.

3

Language Certification

Intensive German training to achieve B2/C1 certification with focus on medical terminology and patient communication.

4

License Recognition

Guidance through the Approbation process - official recognition of medical qualifications by German authorities.

5

Job Placement

Matching with healthcare institutions based on specialty and preferences. Interview facilitation and contract negotiation.

6

Integration Support

Complete relocation support including accommodation, workplace orientation, and ongoing assistance during first months.
