Your Professional Journey to Germany

Our structured 6-step process ensures successful placement and seamless integration into the German healthcare system

1

Application & Document Review

Submit your complete medical qualifications, work experience, and career preferences. Our team conducts a thorough evaluation of your professional background and assesses compatibility with German healthcare standards and requirements.

2

Professional Assessment & Interviews

Participate in comprehensive interviews conducted in Portuguese, English, and basic German. We evaluate your medical knowledge, communication skills, and cultural adaptability for the German healthcare environment.

3

German Language Certification

Complete intensive German language training to achieve B2/C1 level certification. Our specialized program focuses on medical terminology, patient communication, and healthcare documentation essential for German medical practice.

4

Medical License Recognition

We guide you through the Approbation process - the official recognition of your medical qualifications by German authorities. This includes document preparation, submission to medical boards, and examination preparation if required.

5

Job Placement & Contract Negotiation

Once language and legal requirements are fulfilled, we match you with suitable healthcare institutions based on your specialty, location preferences, and career goals. We facilitate interviews and assist with contract negotiations.

6

Relocation & Professional Integration

Complete relocation support including flight arrangements, airport pickup, accommodation setup, and comprehensive workplace orientation. Our team ensures smooth transition and provides ongoing support during your first months in Germany.

Requirements & Qualifications

Clear requirements for healthcare professionals and comprehensive services for medical institutions

For Healthcare Professionals

Educational Background

Medical degree from recognized Brazilian institution, valid medical license (CRM), minimum 2 years of clinical experience in your specialty

Language Requirements

Commitment to achieve B2/C1 German language certification with specialized focus on medical terminology and patient communication

Professional Commitment

Minimum 2-year employment contract commitment, flexibility to work in different German states, cultural adaptability

Required Documentation

Complete CV, certified diploma copies, medical license, criminal background check, health certificate, passport copies

Additional Qualifications

Specialized training certificates, research publications, continuing education records (advantageous but not mandatory)
